How it works

From unboxing to a blinking circuit in your first session.

1

Get your kit

An Arduino UNO, a breadboard, and a handful of components. Affordable and available everywhere.

2

Build the circuit

Follow the step-by-step wiring guide with a clear circuit diagram. No soldering, no guesswork.

3

Write the code

Drag blocks in the browser-based editor. Watch real Arduino code appear as you build.

4

Upload & run it

One click sends your code to the Arduino. Watch your circuit come to life — you built that.

Requires Chrome or Edge · Windows 10/11
