🔧 Build: The Magical Harp

Now it is time to bring your magical harp to life. On this page, you will build the circuit and code the instrument so it can listen for your hand and play different sounds. 🎶

The ultrasonic sensor checks how far away your hand is. Then your Arduino uses that distance to choose what tone the buzzer should play.

🧠 How the System Works

  • 👋 SENSE → The sensor looks for your hand in the air

  • 📏 MEASURE → The Arduino figures out the distance

  • 🎼 CHOOSE → The code picks a tone based on that distance

  • 🔊 PLAY → The buzzer makes the note you selected

🔁 The Flow of Your Program

SENSE HAND → MEASURE DISTANCE → CHOOSE NOTE → PLAY SOUND
