🎻 Mission Briefing: The Magical Harp

You are a magical musician with a very special mission. In this lesson, you will build a harp that sings when your hand moves through the air. ✨

This is not a normal instrument with strings you can touch. Your Arduino will use a distance sensor like magic eyes to watch your hand and choose different notes. 🎵

When your hand moves closer or farther away, the sound will change. That means you can play music without pressing or touching anything at all.

Get ready to build your Invisible Magical Harp and perform your first floating concert. 🌟

🎯 Your magical harp will:

Measure how far away your hand is, choose a sound, and play notes through a buzzer.

🛠 What You Will Learn

  • How a sensor can measure distance
  • How a buzzer can make musical sounds
  • How code can change sounds using numbers
  • How moving your hand can control a project
