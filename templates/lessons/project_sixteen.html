🚨 Emergency Mission!

The head engineer has a BIG problem. The new drones keep their lights ON all the time — they never blink! 🛸

The pilots can't tell if the drone is really on. You have been called in to fix the code! 🔧

New ideas 💭

💡 HIGH and LOW — On and Off

When you write HIGH, the LED turns ON. When you write LOW, it turns OFF.

To make a light blink, you need BOTH — just like flicking a light switch up AND down! 🔄

⏱️ delay() — The Waiting Game

delay(1000) tells your Arduino to wait 1 second before doing the next thing.

Think of it like counting to one before flipping the switch again! 🕐

🔍 Be a Code Detective

When code is broken, read it line by line and look for clues. 🕵️

Ask yourself: "Does every ON have an OFF?"
