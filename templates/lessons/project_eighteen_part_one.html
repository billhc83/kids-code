Welcome to the Drag Strip! 🏎️

The engines are roaring and the crowd is cheering! 🏁 Today YOU are the chief engineer at our very own drag strip speed lab!

You are going to build a real speed trap using two ultrasonic sensors. 📡 When something zooms through, your tracker will clock its speed — just like at a real race!

Grab a toy car, roll a ball, or push anything you want. 🚗 We are going to find out what is the FASTEST thing at the drag strip!

🎯 Your Mission

  • 🔧 Build a two-sensor speed trap on your Arduino
  • 📡 Detect objects passing through Sensor 1, then Sensor 2
  • ⚡ Calculate the speed and show it on screen!

🛠 What You Will Learn

  • How ultrasonic sensors detect nearby objects
  • How to measure time between two events
  • How to calculate speed using distance and time
  • How to show your results on the Serial Monitor
  • How real speed traps work in the world!

⬅️ Head to the build station to set up your speed trap!
